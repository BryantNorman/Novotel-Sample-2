Facilities include experienced event staff, high speed WiFi access, fully air- conditioned rooms and high quality audio visual equipment. Located next to Edinburgh Park train station and directly off the M8, Novotel Edinburgh Park benefits from exceptional access. Edinburgh Airport and City Centre can be accessed via regular buses or trams.
